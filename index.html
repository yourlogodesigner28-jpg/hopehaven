ABOUT HOPE HAVEN

Founded in 2005, Hope Haven has been a beacon of light for orphaned and vulnerable children worldwide. We believe every child deserves a loving home, quality education, and the opportunity to build a bright future.

Our mission is simple yet powerful: to transform lives through compassionate care, educational opportunities, and sustainable support systems that empower children to break the cycle of poverty.

With dedicated teams across 42 countries, we've helped over 5,200 children find hope, healing, and a path to success. But our work is far from over.
